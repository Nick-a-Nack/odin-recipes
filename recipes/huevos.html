Huevos Rancheros

Recipe from SimplyRecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 can of whole or crushed tomatoes, or 1 to 2 large fresh tomatoes when in season
  • 1/2 can (6 ounces) of diced green Anaheim chiles
  • Chipotle chili powder, adobo sauce, or ground cumin to taste, optional
  • 4 Corn Tortillas
  • Butter
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons of Fresh Cilantro, chopped, optional

Recipe

    Making the Salsa

  1. Saute onions in light olive oil in large skillet on medium heat.
  2. Add tomatoes and their juices to onions when they're translucent.
  3. If you are using whole canned tomatoes, break up with fingers or spatula as you put them in the pan. If you are using fresh tomatoes, chop them before adding.
  4. Add chopped green chilies. Add additional seasoning to taste. (Chili powder, adobo sauce, cumin, etc.)
  5. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, and let simmer while you cook the rest, stirring occasionally. Reduce to warm after 10 minutes.

    6. Warm the Plates

  6. Heat oven to 150 degrees Fahreinheit.
  7. Place serving plates in the oven to keep warm.

    8. Prepare Tortillas

  8. Heat a teaspoon of olive oil in a large non-stick skillet on medium high, coating the pan with oil. Heat tortillas individually for a minute or two on each side until softened and air pockets bubble up.
  9. Remove and stack each rortillas on one of the warming plates in the oven to keep warm while you continue cooking the rest of the tortillas and eggs.

    10. Fry the Eggs

  10. Using the same skillet, add about two teaspoons of butter for four eggs.
  11. Heat the pan on medium high heat. Crack 4 eggs into the skillet and cook 3-4 minutes for runny, a minute or two longer for firmer eggs.

    12. Assemble and Serve

  12. To serve, spoon a little sauce onto warmed plate. Top with tortilla, then a fried egg. Top with some more sauce & cilantro.
  13. Serve either one or two eggs and tortillas per plate, depending on appetite.
