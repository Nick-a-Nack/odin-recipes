Spaghetti with Meatballs

Recipe from Parsnip app

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Spaghetti
  • Water
  • 1/4 cup pf Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Tablespoons of Salt
  • 7 oz of Tomato Puree
  • 2 tablespoons (34 grams) of Tomato Paste
  • 2 tablespoons of Cooking Oil
  • 3 Crushed cloves of Garlic
  • 2 sprigs of Basil
  • 2 tablespoons of Butter
  • 10 oz of Ground Beef
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 cup of Breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 of Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of Dried Oregano

Recipe

  1. Add 10oz ground beef, 1 egg, 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs, 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese and 1 tablespoon oregano to a bowl and combine well with gloved hands.
  2. Form beef mix into even 1.5 inch bals by rolling between the hands.
  3. Heat a cast iron or non-stick to medium-high and brown the meatballs in 1 tablespoon of oil, searing them on each side and being careful not to break them.
  4. Once they are browned on the outside but still pink in the middle, place them aside.
  5. Add remaining oil to meatball pan and saute with crushed garlic.
  6. Before garlic darkens, add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste and saute until bubbling, scraping meatball fond.
  7. Deglaze pan with water before adding tomato puree, simmer with lid off stirring when needed.
  8. After simmering and reduced until thick, season with salt to taste and add meatballs to warm them while continuing to simmer.
  9. Add salt to large pot of water and bring to a boil with the lid on.
  10. Once water is at a rolling boil, add pasta and stir frequently in the beginning to prevent sticking.
  11. Once noodles are cooked, strain in sink.
  12. Stir some chopped basil leaves through the sauce.
  13. Serve the sauce and meatballs over spaghetti, sprinkling parmesan over the top and garnish with basil leaves.
