Mushroom & Cheese Omelette

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 3/4 Cup of Mixed Shredded Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons of Unsalted Butter
  • 3/4 cup of Sliced Mushrooms
  • Salt and Pepper to preferred taste

Recipe

  1. Preheat small and medium sized pans at low-medium heat (3/4 on dial).
  2. Mix together eggs and milk with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Once both pans are heated, put 1 Tablespoon of butter into large pan and 1/2 tablespoon of butter into small pan coating evenly.
  4. Once both pants are heated, put mushrooms into small pan and eggs into medium pan.
  5. Pinch some salt and pepper in with mushrooms as they cook. You can flip over larger slices of mushrooms too.
  6. Once eggs have cooked through, turn heat down a smidge and throw most of your shredded cheese onto one half of the omelette.
  7. Once mushrooms are cooked, place them on top of shredded cheese.
  8. Flip the omelette over once eggs are completely solidified.
  9. Let cook for a couple minutes, then flip over to cook opposite side.
  10. I like to serve with toast and coffee!
